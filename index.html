About Me

I’m a cybersecurity enthusiast with a strong interest in ethical hacking, red teaming, and security research. What began as curiosity has grown into a focused effort to understand how systems work, where they break, and how to secure them.

Currently working as an InfoSec Intern at TEPL Hosur, I'm gaining hands-on experience through Capture The Flag competitions, bug bounty programs, and real-world vulnerability research.

With a background in Mechatronics, I bring a unique perspective especially in hardware, embedded systems, and OT/IoT/IIoT security where digital meets physical.

Alongside security, I’ve explored creative problem-solving as a No-Code Web Developer—translating ideas into live projects by guiding AI tools with clear direction and intent.
