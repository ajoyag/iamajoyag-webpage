About Me

I’m a cybersecurity enthusiast with a strong interest in ethical hacking, red teaming, and security research. What began as curiosity has grown into a focused effort to understand how systems work, where they break, and how to secure them.

Currently working as an InfoSec Intern at TEPL Hosur, I’m gaining hands-on experience through Capture The Flag competitions, bug bounty programs, and continuous research into real-world vulnerabilities.

With a background in Mechatronics, I bring a unique perspective to the field—especially in areas like hardware, embedded systems, and OT/IoT/IIoT security, where the digital meets the physical.

Alongside security, I’ve also explored creative problem-solving as a No-Code Web Developer—translating ideas into live projects by guiding AI tools with clear direction and intent.

I’m working to build a career that bridges curiosity and impact—securing systems, solving problems, and constantly learning along the way.
